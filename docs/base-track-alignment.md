### 3. Solving Poor Onboarding & Accessibility
Traditional crypto escrow is a retail nightmare. Valyra improves accessibility through:
*   **Mobile-First Design:** Optimized for the 90% of Indonesian users who are mobile-only.
*   **Smart Wallet / Passkey Support:** We implemented a custom encryption fallback (Ephemeral Keypairs) specifically so users with **Coinbase Passkey Wallets** can use the app without ever needing to see a private key or seed phrase.
